About me



As an embedded software engineer, I've essentially worked in the aeronautical field for the past 15 years (2005 - 2020), whether for civil or military aviation.

I've mainly evolved as a software developer but I was also able to take responsibility as a project manager (1 year) or as a substitute Scrum Master (several weeks).

Now, I'd like to make a career change : leave the world of aeronautics to discover other business segments, whether it be digital, health, environment, machine learning or artificial intelligence.

That's the reason why I've recently (April - May 2020) completed an online course on Python language, with great introductions to various career fields : web development, data science, machine learning, automation, ...

To go even further in my career objective, I've just begun a new online course that will allow me to gain the required skills to work as a web developer : HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, ...

I'm looking forward to applying these new skills in a professional context so I'm open to new job opportunities.
